And over the last century, technology has been a hidden force driving how we experience stories. Most of the beloved and impactful stories would not exist without a major technological breakthrough.

Technology in Storytelling

1890s – Lumière Brothers invent the Cinématographe

Led to the first public motion picture screenings, combining camera, film processor, and projector in one portable machine.

It launched the modern film industry, allowing movies to be seen by audiences for the first time and inspiring global cinema.

1930’s-Disney invent multiplane camera

Led to the first full-color animated films like Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs and early Mickey Mouse cartoons.

Revolutionized animation by adding depth and realism to 2D films.

1940s – Four-Color Rotary Letterpress Invented

Made it possible to mass-produce comic books with vibrant, dynamic artwork. Helped launch Marvel and DC Comics, sparking the superhero boom.

1980s – Pixar creates RenderMan

Created groundbreaking rendering software that eventually led to the release of Toy Story, the first full-length 3D animated movie.

Since then, it powered visuals in Avatar, Lord of the Rings, Finding Nemo and thousands more. Became the gold standard for VFX and CGI in film.

2000s
– Netflix and  Streaming

Redefined entertainment delivery by making on-demand digital content mainstream. Disrupted TV and movie industries, paving the way for streaming ,binge culture and global content access.

Q: What is a SAFE?

A SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity) is a legal agreement giving investors the right to future equity in the company when we raise an official priced round. It’s a common early-stage investment instrument used by startups to raise capital.

Q: Is this a public offering?

No. This is a private investment opportunity offered under applicable securities exemptions. Details provided after submitting an interest form.

Q: When will my investment convert into equity?

Your SAFE investment converts during a future priced round, typically when we secure larger institutional funding.

Q: Why invest now?

You secure discounted equity access before future valuations increase, and gain exclusive perks unavailable in future rounds.

Q: Can I request a refund before signing?

Yes. Your funds are fully refundable until the SAFE agreement is signed. After signing, funds are officially invested and non-refundable.

Q: Will there be other investment rounds?

Yes, but this is our only pre-launch round at this valuation cap. Future rounds will be at a higher valuation.
